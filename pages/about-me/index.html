About me¶

My name is Shane M. Wilkins. I'm really interested in data science topics, finance, government, and philosophy.

I'm currently a management analyst with the USDA, but this is my own personal blog, and the views expressed here are my own, and not the views of the US Federal government or USDA specifically.

Before coming to work in government, I was a postdoc in philosophy at Fordham University and I published a couple of papers

I am a member of the American Philosophical Association's Non-Academic Careers Committee.

I was a Presidential Management Fellow with the USDA and I'm still an ambassador for the program.
